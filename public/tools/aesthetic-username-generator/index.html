Tips for Choosing Aesthetic Usernames

Keep your username short (6-15 characters) for best readability. Choose one font style rather than mixing multiple for a cleaner look. Test your aesthetic name on the target platform before committing, as some platforms filter certain Unicode characters. If a platform strips your fancy text, try a different style -- small caps and bold text tend to have the broadest compatibility.

What To Check Before You Paste

  • Compatibility: preview the output on the platform where you will use it, especially on mobile.
  • Readability: use decorative Unicode for short emphasis, names, labels, and separators rather than long paragraphs.
  • Searchability: keep hashtags, handles, important keywords, and contact information in plain text when discovery matters.
  • Accessibility: screen readers and older devices may announce or display styled Unicode differently from normal text.
