Small Text Generator - Tiny Fonts Copy and Paste

Our small text generator converts your regular-sized text into tiny Unicode characters that appear smaller than standard text. With three distinct small text styles available -- superscript, subscript, and small caps -- you can create unique-looking text for social media bios, creative posts, and anywhere you want compact, eye-catching text.

Three Small Text Styles

Superscript (ˢᵘᵖᵉʳˢᶜʳⁱᵖᵗ) positions characters above the normal text baseline, similar to mathematical exponents. Subscript (ₛᵤᵦₛ꜀ᵣᵢₚₜ) places characters below the baseline, like chemical formulas. Small Caps (Sᴍᴀʟʟ Cᴀᴘꜱ) creates uppercase-shaped letters at lowercase height, producing a sophisticated, typographic look commonly seen in printed materials.

Perfect for Social Media Bios

Small text is incredibly popular for Instagram bios and TikTok profiles because it creates visual contrast. You can use normal-sized text for your main message and small text for secondary details, creating a clean, organized bio layout that stands out from profiles using only regular text.

Limitations to Know

Not every letter has a Unicode superscript or subscript equivalent. Our generator uses the best available match for each character, but some letters (particularly in subscript) may remain at normal size. Small caps offers the most complete coverage of the three styles. Digits 0-9 are well-supported in both superscript and subscript.

What To Check Before You Paste

  • Compatibility: preview the output on the platform where you will use it, especially on mobile.
  • Readability: use decorative Unicode for short emphasis, names, labels, and separators rather than long paragraphs.
  • Searchability: keep hashtags, handles, important keywords, and contact information in plain text when discovery matters.
  • Accessibility: screen readers and older devices may announce or display styled Unicode differently from normal text.
