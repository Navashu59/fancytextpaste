What Are Unicode Symbols?

Unicode is the universal character encoding standard that assigns a unique number to every character, symbol, and emoji used in digital text. Unlike images or custom fonts, Unicode symbols are pure text. They are recognized by every modern device, browser, and application. When you copy a symbol from this page, you are copying a real text character that will display correctly on any platform, from an iPhone to a Windows PC to an Android phone.
