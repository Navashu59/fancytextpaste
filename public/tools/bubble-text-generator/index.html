Bubble Text Generator - Circled Letters Copy and Paste

Our bubble text generator transforms your text into Unicode enclosed characters where each letter appears inside a circle, square, or parentheses. These playful "bubble letters" are one of the most visually distinctive text styles available, making them perfect for creative social media posts, unique bios, and eye-catching messages.

Five Enclosed Styles

Bubble (White Circled) (ⓗⓔⓛⓛⓞ) places each letter inside a white circle outline -- the classic bubble letter look. Black Bubble (🅗🅔🅛🅛🅞) puts letters in filled black circles for a bolder appearance. Square (🄷🄴🄻🄻🄾) encloses letters in white squares. Black Square (🅷🅴🅻🅻🅾) uses filled square enclosures. Parenthesized (⒣⒠⒧⒧⒪) wraps each letter in parentheses.

Platform Compatibility

White circled letters (ⓐⓑⓒ) have excellent compatibility across all platforms. Filled circles and squares (🅐🅑🅒 / 🅰🅱🅲) may render as colored emoji on some systems, which can add a fun, colorful effect but may look different across devices. Test on your target platform for best results.

Creative Uses

  • Social media bios - Spell out your name or key words in bubbles
  • Bullet points - Use individual bubble letters as creative bullet markers
  • Headings - Create distinctive section headers in plain text
  • Gaming profiles - Stand out with bubble letter usernames

What To Check Before You Paste

  • Compatibility: preview the output on the platform where you will use it, especially on mobile.
  • Readability: use decorative Unicode for short emphasis, names, labels, and separators rather than long paragraphs.
  • Searchability: keep hashtags, handles, important keywords, and contact information in plain text when discovery matters.
  • Accessibility: screen readers and older devices may announce or display styled Unicode differently from normal text.
