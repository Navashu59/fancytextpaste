Demonic Text Generator - Evil Gothic & Zalgo Text

The demonic text generator merges two of Unicode's darkest aesthetics into one sinister tool. It combines Gothic Fraktur blackletter, the ornate medieval script used in ancient manuscripts and metal band logos, with the chaotic distortion of Zalgo combining marks that corrupt text into something that looks genuinely otherworldly. The result is text that appears to have been pulled from a forbidden grimoire, each character dripping with dark energy and overflowing with supernatural chaos.

Gothic Fraktur Meets Zalgo Chaos

Gothic Fraktur characters come from the Unicode Mathematical Alphanumeric Symbols block, preserving the look of medieval blackletter calligraphy that dominated European manuscripts for centuries. These angular, ornate letterforms carry an inherent sense of age, authority, and darkness. When layered with Zalgo combining diacritical marks that stack above, through, and below each character, the text transforms from merely medieval into something actively menacing. The combining marks make the already dramatic letterforms appear to bleed, fracture, and spread beyond their boundaries.
