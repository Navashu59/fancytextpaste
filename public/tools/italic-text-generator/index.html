How Italic Unicode Characters Work

The italic characters used by this generator come from the Mathematical Alphanumeric Symbols block in Unicode (range U+1D400-U+1D7FF). Originally designed for mathematical notation, these characters include italic, bold, bold-italic, and script variants of the Latin alphabet. Since they are standard Unicode code points, they display consistently across all modern devices and operating systems — no special fonts or apps required.
