Unicode Text Styles: The Ultimate Reference

This guide is the complete, searchable index of every Unicode text style and fancy font available for use online. Unlike traditional fonts, these styles work by using specific blocks of characters within the Unicode standard that are designed to look like bold, italic, script, or other stylized versions of the alphabet. This means they can be copied and pasted anywhere Unicode is supported, from social media bios to gaming usernames.

How to Use This Interactive List

Type any word or phrase into the input field above. The interactive grid below will instantly show you how that text looks in every available Unicode style. Click on any style's "Copy" button to instantly copy the converted text to your clipboard. You can then paste it directly into Discord, Instagram, TikTok, Roblox, or any other app.

Major Unicode Style Categories

The styles are organized into logical categories. Mathematical Styles (like Double-Struck and Script) come from Unicode's Mathematical Alphanumeric Symbols block. Stylistic Variants (like Small Caps) use the Phonetic Extensions block. Novelty & Symbol Styles (like Cursive or Parenthesized) pull from various symbol and enclosed character blocks to create unique visual effects.

Platform Compatibility

Most modern platforms have excellent Unicode support. Discord, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, and Roblox display nearly all these styles correctly. Some older systems or specific games with custom fonts may show placeholder squares (□) for the fancier styles. When in doubt, test with a short phrase first.
