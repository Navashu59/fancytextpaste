How to Change Your Fortnite Display Name

Follow these steps to set a fancy font as your Fortnite name:

  1. Type your desired Fortnite name in the generator above.
  2. Browse through the font styles and pick your favorite.
  3. Click the copy button to copy the styled text.
  4. Go to epicgames.com and log into your account.
  5. Navigate to Account Settings and find the Display Name field.
  6. Clear your current name and paste the fancy text.
  7. Click Save Changes to update your Fortnite display name.

Your new stylized name will appear across all Epic Games titles, including Fortnite Battle Royale, Creative, and Save the World. Remember that display name changes are limited to once every 14 days.
