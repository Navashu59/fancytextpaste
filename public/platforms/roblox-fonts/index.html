How to Change Your Roblox Display Name with Fancy Text

Follow these steps to set a fancy display name on Roblox:

  1. Type your desired name in the generator above.
  2. Browse through the font styles and find one you like.
  3. Click the copy button next to your chosen style.
  4. Open Roblox and go to Settings (gear icon).
  5. Click on your Display Name field.
  6. Clear the existing name and paste your fancy text.
  7. Click Save to apply your new display name.

Remember that Roblox display names can be changed once every 7 days, so choose a style you love. Your username remains unchanged and is used for login.

Unicode Support on Roblox

Roblox supports Unicode characters in display names, group names/descriptions, and game titles/descriptions. The platform's font stack includes Segoe UI, Arial, and system fallbacks, rendering Unicode mathematical alphanumeric symbols (U+1D400–U+1D7FF), letterlike symbols (U+2100–U+214F), and other supplemental Unicode blocks. Display name changes are rate‑limited (once per 7 days) and enforce a 20‑character limit with Unicode code‑point counting.

Roblox Safety & Filtering

Roblox's in‑game chat filter blocks most non‑ASCII Unicode characters for safety, preventing font styles in chat messages. However, display names and descriptive fields are exempt. The filter uses a combination of keyword detection, Unicode category exclusions, and pattern matching; Mathematical Alphanumeric Symbols pass through for display purposes.
